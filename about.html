About Us

TRUST & FRIENDS
FOUNDATION

2 Davis Ekpo Street , Abak LGA

We engage in integrated agricultural production/ processing, as well as in commercial, industrial, or fiscal activity which is in the best judgment of the society can profitably be undertaken for the benefit of the stakeholders/members in accordance with cooperative principles.
We raise funds/credit that would be made available to members as loans at fair and reasonable rates of interest for productive purposes.
We enhance most effectively economic, financial and human resources of the members through active participation of members in the general economic and social development of the society.
We also enter in any contract or arrangement with any local, state or federal government authorities, companies or firms or persons, whether formal or informal, frights, interest, privileges and concessions which the society may think desirable.
We also serve as platform for acquiring entrepreneurship skill/crafts, and training for the benefits of members etc.
We promote welfare scheme for members.
We affiliate/partner the society with other corporate organizations/agency whose ideas and policies are identical with the objectives of this society.
